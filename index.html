Crucider - Wassail!


The intention was to stimulate the making of cider communally within parishes, by drawing together a “diffuse” orchard from contributing gardens. The cider would then be shared out, or sold to raise funds for parish causes and concerns.


There has been some success with this. The increased discipline of a monastic approach is now needed.
